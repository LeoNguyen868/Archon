# Research Skill
Skill for searching and looking up information from the codebase or external documentation.

## When to Use
- When starting a new task that requires understanding context.
- When encountering new technologies or unclear APIs.
- When finding similar implementations in the codebase is needed.

## Instructions
- **Internal Search:** Use `grep`, `find`, `codebase_search` to search within the project.
- **External Search:** (If allowed) Look up docs, references.
- **Analyze:** Read and understand the found code/docs.
- **Synthesize:** Summarize findings, examples, and caveats.
- **Output:** Research findings document or summary.
